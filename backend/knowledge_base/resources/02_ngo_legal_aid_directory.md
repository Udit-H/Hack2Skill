## 1. Legal Aid — Government

### Karnataka State Legal Services Authority (KSLSA)
- **Address**: Nyaya Degula, High Court of Karnataka Complex, Bengaluru - 560001
- **Phone**: 080-22113824, 080-22113800
- **Website**: kslsa.kar.nic.in
- **Services**: Free legal aid, Lok Adalat, mediation, legal literacy
- **Eligibility**: Income < ₹3 lakh/year, women, SC/ST, senior citizens, disabled

### National Legal Services Authority (NALSA)
- **Helpline**: 15100 (toll-free)
- **Website**: nalsa.gov.in
- **Tele-law**: Free legal advice via video call for rural citizens
- **How**: Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) for Tele-law consultation

## 4. Shelter Homes

### One-Stop Centres (Sakhi) — All Districts
- **How to access**: Call **181** or walk in
- **Services**: Medical, legal, police facilitation, psycho-social counselling, temporary shelter
- **Available in**: All 31 districts of Karnataka

### Swadhar Greh (Long-term shelter)
- Run by government + NGO partners
- Shelter for up to 3 years with skill training
- Contact through District DWCD office
